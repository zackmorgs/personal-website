The HOKA ONE ONE Challenger ATR 2 is the new version of the original Challenger with a more supportive fit, made for running on both road and trail.

HOKA ONE ONE Challenger ATR 2 Grey/Citrus Mesh Men's Shoe

Features include:

  • Closed Air Mesh Upper Provides Added Protection on the Trail
  • Seamless No-Sew Midfoot Support
  • Ortholite Insole
  • Early Stage Meta-Rocker
  • Oversize EVA Midsole Provides Lightweight Cushioning
  • 4mm Lugs Provide All-Terrain Traction
  • Podular Outsole Design Provides Stability on Uneven Terrain
  • Offset: 5mm
  • Heel: 29mm
  • Forefoot: 24mm
  • WEIGHTS: 9.5 oz / 270g

  • Gender: Men's
  • Material: Mesh
