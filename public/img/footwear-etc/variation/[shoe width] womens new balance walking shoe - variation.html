The New Balance WW928WT White Leather Women's Shoe updates the 927, with an improved ROLLBAR and now with E-NERGY for a more responsive ride. The WW928 is the lighter weight and flexible leather and mesh version of the all-leather 928. The perfect shoe for large frame people on their feet all day, the 928 motion control walking shoe is the choice for those seeking the utmost stability and cushioning such as maximum over-pronators. Approved by Medicare for diabetics.

New Balance WW928 WT White Leather Women's Shoe

Features include:

  • ABZORB shock absorption
  • Cushioning & flexibility
  • Leather/Mesh Upper
  • Medicare Approved
  • Ndurance rubber
  • N-ERGY midsole
  • Non-marking rubber
  • Odor resistant
  • Premium foam insert
  • ROLLBAR motion control
  • Seamless phantom liner
  • Stabilizing outsole
  • Medicare/HCPCS code = A5500 Diabetic Shoe
