This Limited Edition MBT style is a Footwear etc. worldwide exclusive! You won't find this special color anywhere else!

MBT's latest version of the ever-popular Sport series! The perfect athletic-styled walking shoe for everyday activities as well as serious exercise. The unique multi-layered sole and Masai sensor emulates the feeling of walking on sand.

The MBT Sport 3 is part of the Extra Comfort Collection which provides an easy fit and extra comfort by offering extra volume.

MBT Sport 3 Pink/White Leather/Mesh Women's Shoe

Features include:

  • Lightweight 3D mesh upper and lining are highly breathable
  • PU coated materials are durable and resistant
  • Dual density midsole is made with TPU and nylon mixed with glass fibers for extra firmness and durability
  • Multi-layered sole creates natural instability to emulate the feeling of walking on sand
  • Durable rubber outsole provides excellent traction
  • Removable insoles so that you can add your own orthotic insoles

The MBT Sport 3 in Pink comes with a free pair of pink shoe laces for those who prefer pink over white.

This MBT style features removable insoles so that you can add your own orthotic insoles. Although to fully benefit from the MBT technology the MBT shoes should be worn without orthotics.

If you have any questions you can find the answers in our MBT Shoes Frequently Asked Questions section.

Wearing MBT Shoes for the first time? Watch the MBT Shoes Instructional Video

The MBT Index simplifies the process of finding the right level of MBT shoe for your needs. It ranks MBT into one of three categories, starting at Activate, our least level of rock, and progressing through Dynamic and ending with our highest level of rock, Performance.
